About Me

I am a Hydrologist, Research Scientist, and Entrepreneur operating at the intersection of **AI, remote sensing, and water sustainability**. As the Co-founder and Innovation Director of Tributary, I lead the development of scalable intelligence technologies that help utilities and managers monitor water resources with unprecedented precision.

My research at Arizona State University focuses on developing deep learning architectures (such as U-Net) to process high-resolution CubeSat imagery.
